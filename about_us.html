About Us

The Greenwood Community Library has been at the heart of our neighborhood for over two decades. More than just a place to borrow books, our library is a welcoming space where people of all ages can come together to learn, share, and connect.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to inspiring a love for reading, fostering lifelong learning, and strengthening community bonds. Our mission is to make knowledge and culture accessible to everyone in Greenwood and beyond.

What We Offer

  • A diverse collection of books, e-books, and audiobooks for all ages
  • Weekly programs such as storytime, book clubs, and workshops
  • Free access to computers, Wi-Fi, and study spaces
  • Community events and cultural celebrations throughout the year

Our Values

At Greenwood Community Library, we value inclusivity, curiosity, and collaboration. We believe libraries are safe havens that encourage creativity and provide opportunities for everyone to grow.

Join Us

Whether you are a lifelong reader, a student, or simply looking for a place to connect with others, our library doors are always open to you. Come explore, discover, and be part of the Greenwood story!
