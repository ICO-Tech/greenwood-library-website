Book Reviews

Welcome to our Book Reviews section! Here, library members and staff share their thoughts on recent reads to help you discover your next favorite book. Explore reviews, ratings, and recommendations from the Greenwood community.

The Curious Garden – by Peter Brown

A heartwarming children’s story about a boy who transforms a gray city into a vibrant garden. Beautiful illustrations and a powerful message about caring for our environment.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5)

Educated – by Tara Westover

A gripping memoir of resilience and determination. Westover’s journey from a survivalist family in rural Idaho to earning a PhD from Cambridge is both inspiring and eye-opening.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

The Midnight Library – by Matt Haig

A moving novel that explores the choices we make and the lives we could have lived. Thought-provoking and uplifting, with a blend of fantasy and philosophy.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5)
