Welcome to the Greenwood Community Library

The Greenwood Community Library is more than just a place to find books — it’s a vibrant hub for learning, creativity, and community connection. Whether you’re stopping by to borrow your favorite novel, attend a workshop, or simply enjoy a quiet reading corner, we’re here to make you feel at home.

What’s New?

Don’t miss our latest community events — from children’s storytime to author meet-and-greets, there’s something for everyone. Visit the Events page to see what’s coming up next.

Join the Greenwood Community

Becoming a library member is free and easy! Gain access to our full collection of books, digital resources, and exclusive events. Visit us in person or get in touch through our Contact Us page to learn more.
